Simple recipe to make one cup of tea:

  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 teaspoon of tea leaves or 1 tea bag
  • 1-2 teaspoon of sugar (optional)
  • 1/4 cup of milk

Instuctions:

  1. Boil the Water: Bring 1 cup of water to a rolling boil.
  2. Add Tea: If using tea leaves, add 1 teaspoon to the boiling water. If using a tea bag, place it in your cup
  3. Steep: Let the tea steep for 3-5 minutes, depending on how strong you like your tea.
  4. Add Sugar and Milk: If you like your tea sweet, add 1-2 teaspoons of sugar. For a creamier tea, add 1/4 cup of milk.
  5. Strain and Serve: If you used tea leaves, strain the tea into your cup. If you used a tea bag, simply remove it from the cup.
  6. Enjoy: Your tea is ready to be enjoyed!
